A little about ourselves

Formerly known as the Subcommittee for the Elimination of Racism, of the NGO Committee for Human Rights, CERAC, the NGO Committee for the Elimination of Racism, Afrophobia & Colorism, was founded in 2000, as a response to the concerns of the World Conference Against Racism, Racial Discrimination, Xenophobia and related Intolerance.  It is and remains the only organisation to consistently advocate for policies that address the scourge of all forms of racism and discrimination at the United Nations. We regularly organise public programs to educate civil society on the history of, and best practices for confronting social intolerance. .

Recent News
The UN Working Group of Experts for People of African Descent issued their preliminary report of the findings of the US five city, country visit, January 12-29, 2016. Members of our Committee were in attendance and our Chair Dòwòti Désir was among the testifiers at the New York City visit.  The Working Group concluded that the US Government does not show any committment towards providing people of African descent with reparations or opportunity to a truth and reconciliations commissions which serves to the detriment of its population of African descendants. The Chairperson of the WGPAD is pictured above: Mirielle Fanon-Mendes-France.  Please see the full report at the OCHCR website.
